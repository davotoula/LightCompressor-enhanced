# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 27, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Light Compressor Enhanced LCe app ("we," "us," "our," or "Application") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play Store.

## Information We Collect

### Analytics and Crash Data

We use Google Firebase to collect analytics data and crash reports to improve our application's quality and performance. This includes:

- **Crash Dumps**: Automatic error and crash logs that help us identify and fix bugs
- **Usage Events**: Information about features you use, session duration, and app interactions
- **Device Information**: Device model, OS version, and app version
- **Performance Metrics**: App performance data to optimize functionality

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Application, we automatically collect:
- Device identifiers
- Operating system information
- App version and installation source
- Approximate location (country/region) based on IP address
- Crash reports and error logs

## How We Use Your Information

We collect and analyze this information to:
- Identify and fix application bugs and crashes
- Improve application performance and stability
- Understand feature usage and user behavior
- Enhance user experience
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Retention

We retain analytics and crash data in accordance with Google Firebase's default retention policies. Firebase typically retains analytics data for 60 days, while other data may be retained longer for analysis and improvement purposes.

## Your Privacy Rights

- **Access**: You may request access to personal data we hold about you
- **Deletion**: You can request deletion of your data
